• Does the camera beep (di-di-di)? If not, reset the camera and make sure that you hear the di-di-di. Then try AirLink again.

  • You have tried 2 times via AirLink, but heard no ‘dong’ sound from the camera? Please try backup options:

    Smart Scan (HiKam S5, Q7, A7 2016 Version)

    AP Mode (HiKam S6, HiKam A7 2018 Version)

  • Some routers offer 2.4GHz WiFi and 5GHz WiFi at the same time. Please make sure your phone is connected to 2.4GHz WiFi, you can check and confirm it on your WiFi router.
    In case of doubt, please disable 5GHz WiFi temporarily. When AirLink is completed, you can enable it again.

  • Power off the WiFi router, wait for one minute, then power on the WiFi router again. Wait for the router to connect to internet, then try AirLink again.

  • Make sure that the WiFi router meets the following requirements:
    • Disable MAC filter (or disable it temporarily until AirLink is completed)
    • DHCP is enabled
    • WiFi encryption：WPA2-PSK or WPA2-AES
    • Disable options on WiFi router: ‘Guest Access’ and ‘Only surf and mail’
    • Change the WiFi channel to ‘automatic’
